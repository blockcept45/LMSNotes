FAQs

What is Saarthi LMS?

Saarthi is a New Gen Learning Management System designed to enhance the educational experience for students, educators, and institutions. Our platform offers a range of tools and features to support personalized learning, collaboration, and effective course management.

For Students

How do I access my courses?
After logging in to your Saarthi LMS account, you can access your courses from the dashboard. All enrolled courses will be listed under the "My Courses" section.

How Can I track my progress?
You can track your progress through your personalized dashboard. It provides an overview of your completed assignments, quiz scores, and overall performance in each course.

What should I do if I forget my password?
If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

For Educators

How do I create a new course?
To create a new course, log in to your educator account, go to the "Course Management" section, and click on "Create New Course." Follow the prompts to add course details, upload content, and set up assessments.

Can I integrate third-party tools into my courses?
Yes, Saarthi LMS supports integration with various third-party tools and platforms, including Google Drive, Microsoft Office, and more. Visit our Integrations Page for a full list of supported tools.

How can I communicate with my students?
You can communicate with your students using the built-in messaging system, discussion forums, and announcement features. These tools help facilitate effective and timely communication.

For Institutions

Is Saarthi LMS scalable for large institutions?
Absolutely. Saarthi LMS is designed to scale according to the needs of your institution, whether you are a small school or a large university. Our platform can handle a large number of users and courses seamlessly.

What kind of support do you offer for institutions?
We offer dedicated support for institutions, including onboarding assistance, training sessions, and a dedicated account manager to ensure a smooth experience.

How secure is our data on Saarthi LMS?
Data security is our top priority. Saarthi LMS employs robust security measures, including data encryption, regular backups, and compliance with international data protection regulations to ensure the safety and privacy of your data.

Technical Support

Are there any system requirements for using Saarthi LMS?
Saarthi LMS is a web-based platform accessible via modern web browsers such as Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge. For the best experience, ensure your browser is up-to-date and you have a stable internet connection.
